THE CHALLENGE

Redesign and Rebrand the Home Page of Homestead High School's Art Department.

Homestead High School is a high school in the Fremont High School District, located in the Bay Area.

DATE

Apr. 2018

SERVICES

Rebranded Graphic Identity, New Interface Design

Graphic Identity

For this project, the goals & needs of the target audience (art students + students interested in joining the art program at Homestead High School) were essential to the site design.

Considering the target audience, I focused on designing a clean, professional page and clarifying navigations by implementing a sleek, modern aesthetic that would appeal to high school students. Information hierarchy was designed on the basis of student interests and needs, beginning with a general navigation at the very top of the page followed by the course list, upcoming events, a gallery showcasing student work, and faculty spotlight.
