about me

I am a hardworking and passionate designer with an avid love for books, music, and coffee! I recently graduated from the University of California, Davis in Spring of 2018 with an emphasis in graphics and a minor in communications.

I feel strongly about creating positive social impact through design and helping the world gain a more accurate perception of how prevalent design is today. I am currently interested in web design and am searching for opportunities to expand my understanding of/experience with UI/UX design.

When I am not designing, you can find me:

  • Searching for new music
  • On the lookout for good coffee
  • Sketching, painting, creating
  • Traveling & creating new experiences
